Paris — The City of Light

A city of romance, history, and breathtaking beauty

Paris is the capital city of France and one of the most visited cities in the world. It sits along the Seine River in northern France and has been a centre of art, fashion, and culture for centuries. Whether you're strolling along the Champs-Élysées, visiting world-class museums, or simply enjoying a croissant at a street café, Paris has something magical to offer every visitor.

The city is home to iconic landmarks that are recognised across the globe. From the towering Eiffel Tower to the grand Notre-Dame Cathedral, Paris blends history with modern life in a way that few cities can match. Its neighbourhoods each have their own character — Montmartre is full of artists and charm, while Le Marais offers trendy boutiques and historic architecture.

Paris is also a paradise for food lovers. The city boasts thousands of restaurants, bakeries, and patisseries serving everything from classic French cuisine to international dishes. A visit to a traditional French brasserie for steak frites or onion soup is an experience you will never forget.
